What to Eat?

You will probably want something to eat along with that. Here are some letters that will come in handy.

CapitalSmallSound
Х х German ch in Bach or Scottish Loch Ness
З з z as in zebra
Ю ю you
Ш ш sh as in ship

And here are some words:

RussianEnglish
меню́ menu
за́втрак breakfast
обе́д lunch
хлеб bread
шокола́д chocolate

Important: This consonant З / з is very easily confused with this vowel Э / э The consonant has a “broken” curve whereas the vowel has a smooth curve. In this font, the difference is quite subtle (especially on the capital letter). In most printed material, the difference will be quite clear.
