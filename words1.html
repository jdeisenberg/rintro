And here are some words that use those letters. See if you can figure out how they’re pronounced before you click the speaker.

WordMeans

To help you with the pronunciation, we put an accent mark on the syllable that gets the emphasis in multi-syllable words. Most beginning textbooks or phrasebooks do this also. You won't see the accent marks in Russia, though.
