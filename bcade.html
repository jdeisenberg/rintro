Just Say "No"

At the right is a picture of a barricade. Whenever you see a barricade, you naturally think of no entry.

At the right is a Russian letter that looks very much like a barricade, and it has the sound of the first consonant in the words no entry.
We use this consonant in the Russian word for "no", spelled like this:
нет  
When you see the letter н, whose Russian name is pronounced "en", make sure you think of the "no entry" barricade -- if you don't, you're playing with the wrong rules!

Here are the individual letters that make up the word:

CapitalSmallSounds like
        n in no
        ye in yet
        t in top
