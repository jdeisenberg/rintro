Left and Right

After asking where the restaurant is:

Где́ рестора́н?

You may hear one of these in response:

To the left.   |   Straight ahead.   |   To the right.  

You’ll need four new letters to write them in Russian. Two of them are ones that we would ordinarily present with “Picture Dramas.” Please make a choice:

  • If you’re thinking in Russian and not trying to apply the rules of English, skip the drama and just see the letters.

  • If you haven’t quite made the switch, or if you just love drama, see the letters complete with Picture Dramas.
