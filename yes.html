Just say “да”

Here’s your first Russian word: the word for “yes.”

да  

And here are the letters individually:

CapitalSmallSounds like
  Д     д   d in dog
  А     а   a in aha!

The second letter (the vowel) is always pronounced like ah. If you try any other pronunciation, you're playing the game with the wrong rules.
