Let's Drink!

Learning the Russian alphabet is thirsty work. We can use the letters we already know to get some drinks at the рестора́н.

вода́ water
молоко́ milk
лимона́д soft drink (despite what it sounds like, this is a generic name for a soft drink!)
квас kvas (This is some sort of fermented drink; I have had some but cannot for the life of me describe the taste.)

Perhaps you're in the mood for something a bit stronger, which you could also buy at a бар:

пи́во beer
вино́ wine
во́дка vodka

No, I haven’t forgotten about coffee and tea; you just don’t have the letters for them yet!
